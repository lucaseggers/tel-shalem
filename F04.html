Mo left at some point for the US, and lived in the Los Angeles area for almost twenty years before coming back. He leaned back and laughed when I told him I was American. “You know,” Mo said, “I actually feel safer here than I did in LA!” I asked why. “Well, here there are tensions between groups sometimes, but there is no random violence, you know? Jerusalem has none of your school shooters, you aren’t killed for living your life.” Mo smiled. “In that way I think here is much more safe.”
