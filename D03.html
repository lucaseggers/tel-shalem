I'd turn a couple corners as we snaked our way further south. Here, a greenhouse with massive whirring ventilation fans, working hard even at five AM. There, a date palm plantation, where unseen workers had stacked up trunks into a giant mosaic of scaly-looking bark. Most of the sounds actually reminded me of home: crunches of rocks under wheels, birdsongs of swallows flittering from tree to tree, and the hums of paddles turning in aquaculture ponds.
